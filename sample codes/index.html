1

Manual Dismantling

Our skilled technicians carefully dismantle all types of electronic devices into their component parts. This includes separating circuit boards, plastics, metals, glass, and batteries.

Specialized tools
Skilled technicians

Safety Note: Batteries are stored in fireproof containers. CRT monitors and TVs are handled only by certified professionals to prevent exposure to harmful materials.

2

Component Testing & Reuse

Each salvageable component undergoes rigorous testing to determine functionality. Working parts like RAM, processors, hard drives, and display panels are cleaned, certified, and prepared for reuse.

ISO 9001 Certified
Electrical safety tests

All data storage devices are securely wiped using DoD-compliant methods to protect previous owners' privacy before testing and resale.

3

Material Recovery

Non-functional components enter our material recovery process, where valuable resources are extracted through mechanical and chemical processes:

  • Circuit boards: Gold, silver, palladium, and copper recovery
  • Wires: Copper extraction after insulation removal
  • Plastics: Granulation and sorting by polymer type
  • Batteries: Safe extraction of lithium, cobalt, and nickel

Note: Chemical processing is only performed by our certified environmental partners to ensure proper handling of hazardous materials.

4

Remanufacturing

Devices with sufficient working components are remanufactured into fully functional products. Our technicians rebuild laptops, desktops, smartphones, and tablets using a combination of salvaged and new parts.

Quality control
90-day warranty

Each refurbished device undergoes comprehensive testing and receives a condition grade (A, B, or C) before being offered for sale at affordable prices.

5

Safe Disposal

Materials that cannot be reused or recycled are disposed of according to strict environmental guidelines. This includes:

  • Mercury-containing components from LCD screens
  • Lead from CRT glass
  • Flame retardants from certain plastics
  • Damaged batteries and capacitors

Our facility is E-Waste Rules 2016 Compliant and adheres to all local and international regulations for electronic waste management.

E-Waste We Process

We handle a wide range of electronic waste types, each with specialized recycling methods

Computers & Laptops

Motherboards, CPUs, RAM, GPUs, power supplies, and casings are dismantled for component recovery and metals extraction.

Mobile Devices

Smartphones and tablets are processed for precious metals, rare earth elements, and battery materials after secure data wiping.

Circuit Boards

PCBs contain high concentrations of valuable metals including gold, silver, and palladium that are recovered through specialized processing.

Wires & Cables

Insulation is removed and copper is recovered through mechanical stripping and granulation processes.

Displays & Monitors

LED, LCD, and CRT displays require specialized handling to safely manage mercury, lead, and other hazardous materials.

Batteries

Lithium-ion, nickel-cadmium, and lead-acid batteries undergo safe dismantling for metal recovery and hazardous material containment.
